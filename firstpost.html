The Materials Used In Basketballs

The main materials in basketballs is leather and rubber. The NBA uses very high quality leather so that the players have a very good grip on the ball. The rubber is used to hold the 8 leather panels together.
In 2008 the NBA changed the way the basketball was made and the materials used to make it. In that time the players were furios players have been reported to say that when traing the new basketball made their skin come off of their fingers and onto the ball.
The machines used to make the basketball are very complicated they are specialy made to place leather panels and in the right spot so that the ball looks and feels perfect.
